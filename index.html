About KU Reads: A Common Book Experience


The KU Reads program selects one Common Book each academic year to inspire campus-wide discussion, intellectual engagement, and promote community across KU. This program provides a unique opportunity to support the student experience by fostering community through engaging programming and supporting intellectual engagement through shared conversations about topics of significance in Kansas and beyond.
Program Goals:
  • Build community among students, faculty, and staff
  • Encourage intellectual engagement through reading, discussion, curriculum, and events
  • Create a shared conversation about topics and issues of significance in today’s world
